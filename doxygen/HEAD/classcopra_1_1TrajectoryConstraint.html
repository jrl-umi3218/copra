Detailed Description

Trajectory constraint class. Depending on the parameter 'isInequalityConstraint' during the construction it can be an Equality constraints ( \(Ex = f\) or \(EX = f\)) or an Inequality constraints ( \(Ex\leq f\) or \(EX\leq f\)) with \(X=[x_1^T ... x_{nrStep}^T]^T\).

Constructor of the trajectory constraint. Create a constraint of type \(Ex\leq f\) or \(Ex = f\) or \(EX\leq f\) or \(EX = f\) with \(X=[x_1^T ... x_N^T]^T\).
As \(U\) is the optimization variable, \(Ex\leq f\) or \(Ex = f\) is transformed to be \(AU\leq b\) or \(AU = b\). Perform a move semantic if an rvalue is given (this is faster).

Parameters
EThe matrix side of the constraint
fThe vector side of the constraint
isInequalityConstraintWhether the constraint is an Inequality (true) or an Equality (false)
